Who are we?

At Vinea Care, our vision is creating a better everyday life for all the people we serve. Our mission, which is the anchor of our organisation, is delivering quality care compassionately. We meet you with an honest greeting and offer an environment that feels like home. By doing this we hope to improve the quality of people's lives. Vinea, which is latin for Vineyard, is where we grew up calling home and learnt the values we now take to others. These values are at the core of our services.

Our well-trained care takers assist with everyday household, personal, social, and medical tasks. We ensure that their life is in good order and that they can enjoy their golden-sunset years in comfort, peace, and joy.
Our services include;

  • Live-in care
  • Respite Care
  • Domiciliary Care
  • Palliative Care
  • Complex Care
