Who are we?

We are England's most trusted live-in care providers. Offering exceptional full-time care with excellence and compassion.

Currently located at 53 The Vineyard in Welwyn Garden City, we wish to spread our Vinea Care vines around the UK, offering our celebrated services to our beloved seniors.

Our well-trained care takers assist with everyday household, personal, social, and medical tasks. We ensure that their life is in good order and that they can enjoy their golden-sunset years in comfort, peace, and joy.
Our services include;

  • Live-in care
  • Respite Care
  • Domiciliary Care
  • Palliative Care
  • And more

We handle the specifics of their everyday life so that you may spend less time running up and down, and more time bonding with them.
